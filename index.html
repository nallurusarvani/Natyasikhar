Welcome to my personal website! I am a passionate dancer and a dedicated student.

About Me

I am Sarvani Nalluru, an accomplished dancer from Repalle, currently pursuing a B.Tech degree at Malineni Lakshmaiah Women's Engineering College. My journey in the arts is complemented by a deep commitment to academics and painting, which I balance with my studies.

Achievements

  • Recognition:I was honored with a certificate of appreciation at the Maha Brunda Natyam during Krishna Pushkara.
  • Competitions:I received accolades at the 'Kala Utsav' programs in Repalle and Bapatla divisions in 2017.
  • Performances: I have given more than 300 performances, showcasing my skills in various significant locations, including Guntur, Krishna, Sattenapalli, Tenali, Bapatla, Vijayawada, and parts of Telangana. My performances in the 'Janmabhoomi - Ma Ooru' programs have also received high praise.
  • Awards:I have secured numerous awards, including:
    • A Gold Medal at the 10th International Youth and Child Art Exhibition.
    • First Prize at the VIVA program organized by VVIT College.
    • Various accolades at college events like Sankranti Sambaralu and Samskruti.
    • Recognitions at several cultural and dance festivals.
  • Notable Praise: I have been commended by prominent figures such as MLA Anagani Sathyaprasad, former MLA Devineni Mallikarjunarao, MP Mopidevi Venkataramana Rao, IAS officer Addanki Sridhar, and Central Minister Pemmasani Chadra Sekhar.

Education

    Following my 10th and 12th standards, I pursued diplomas in Satvikabhinayam and Yakshaganam to deepen my expertise in Kuchipudi. Now, as a B.Tech student at Malineni Lakshmaiah Women's Engineering College, I actively participate in various college events such as:

    • Sankranti Sambaralu:Celebrating the Sankranti festival.
    • Samskruti:A cultural event organized by the college, featuring both traditional and contemporary performances
    • VIVA Program:I won first prize at this event hosted by VVIT College.

    My goal is to promote Indian culture through my dance while successfully managing my academic and artistic pursuits.

Training

  • Kuchipudi: I have received extensive training under the esteemed Kuchipudi dancer Vedantam Durga Bhavani Garu for nine years.
  • Additional Training: I have completed diplomas in Satvikabhinayam and Yakshaganam, further honing my skills in Kuchipudi.

    • Commitment to Indian Culture

    I am dedicated to fostering a greater interest in Kuchipudi dance and am grateful for the invaluable support of my parents, Balasri and Nalluru Venkata Sridhar, who have been instrumental in my journey to organize and perform shows.
